About Me

Where Code Meets Creativity & Calling

Rodney Gonzales is a multi-disciplinary professional whose life's work sits at the intersection of technology, music, and faith. As a Music Director and Audio Engineer, he crafts sonic experiences that move audiences — from intimate church gatherings to full live productions.

As an IT Professor, Rodney equips the next generation of technologists with skills in programming, systems, and research. His industry experience as a Web Application Developer specializing in CRM systems and as an iOS Mobile Developer keeps his teaching grounded in real-world practice.

As a Youth Pastor, he channels leadership and empathy into mentoring young people — helping them navigate life, faith, and their own potential with confidence and purpose.
